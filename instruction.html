JEE Mains 2025 Score Calculator

Instructions

  1. Open the JEE Mains 2025 Score Calculator website.
  2. Select your exam date and shift from the dropdown menu.
  3. Paste the URL of your response sheet (must start with https://cdn3.digialm.com) for the best experience.
  4. Alternatively, upload your response sheet as an .html file (not recommended, as question and option images may not be fetched).
  5. Click "Evaluate" to process your response sheet and calculate your score.
  6. View your total score, subject-wise breakdown (Physics, Chemistry, Maths), and detailed question-wise analysis.
  7. Use the "Show Only Incorrect Questions" toggle to filter incorrect answers, or click on thumbnails to view question or option images in full size.

Privacy Notice

  • Your application number, name, or personal information is not stored or shared.
  • Only your score and exam shift are stored in the database. If enough scores are collected, this data will be used to improve percentile predictions on link.com, making them more accurate.
